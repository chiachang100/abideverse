## 📝 Study Methods

### Inductive Bible Study (OIA)

1. **Observation** - What does the text say?
2. **Interpretation** - What does it mean?
3. **Application** - How should I respond?

### SOAP Method

- **S**cripture - Write the verse
- **O**bservation - What stands out?
- **A**pplication - How to live it out?
- **P**rayer - Respond to God

### Lectio Divina (Divine Reading)

1. **Lectio** (Read) - Slow, attentive reading
2. **Meditatio** (Reflect) - Ponder the meaning
3. **Oratio** (Respond) - Pray the text
4. **Contemplatio** (Rest) - Rest in God's presence

## 📊 Bible Reading Plans

### Chronological

### 5x5x5 Plan

- Read 1 chapter daily (5 minutes)
- 5 days per week
- Through the New Testament in 1 year

Intensive plan reading about 12 pages daily
