# 🙏 About AbideVerse

**AbideVerse 常在主裡** – Your Daily Bible Verse Companion

_"Abide in me, and I in you."_ — John 15:4

---

## 📖 Our Mission

AbideVerse exists to help you **abide in God's Word** daily. We believe that spending time with Scripture transforms lives, brings peace to anxious hearts, and deepens our relationship with Christ.

In a world of constant distractions, AbideVerse provides a quiet space where you can:

- **Reflect** on meaningful Bible verses
- **Find joy** in God's promises
- **Discover treasures** of wisdom from centuries of Christian thought
- **Grow** in your faith, one verse at a time

---

## ✨ Features

### 📜 Daily Scriptures

Curated Bible verses organized by theme, season, and spiritual need. Each verse comes with context and reflection questions to help you apply God's Word to your life.

### 😊 Joys

Uplifting devotional content that points you to the goodness of God. Perfect for starting your day with a renewed perspective.

### 💎 Treasures

Timeless wisdom from church history, including quotes, hymns, and insights from spiritual giants like Augustine, Luther, Wesley, and modern voices.

### 🌍 Multi-Language Support

Available in English, Traditional Chinese (繁體中文), and Simplified Chinese (简体中文) – with more languages coming soon.

### 🔖 Favorites

Save your most meaningful verses, joys, and treasures for easy access later.

### 🎯 Daily Reminders

Optional notifications to help you build a consistent Bible reading habit.

---

## 🎯 Why "AbideVerse"?

The name combines two powerful ideas:

**"Abide"** – From John 15:4, Jesus calls us to remain, dwell, and live in Him. Abiding isn't a one-time decision but a daily choice to stay connected to the Vine.

**"Verse"** – Scripture verses are the building blocks of our faith. Each verse carries divine power to comfort, correct, and inspire.

**"AbideVerse"** = A space where you abide in God's Word daily.

The Chinese name **「常在主裡」** captures the same essence – always remaining in the Lord.

Everything we create is rooted in the Bible. We don't add opinions – we point to God's Word.

### 🤝 Accessible

We believe everyone should have access to quality biblical content – regardless of language, background, or financial situation. AbideVerse will always be **free**.

## 🛠️ Technology

AbideVerse is built with:

- **Flutter** – For a beautiful, smooth experience on iOS, Android, and Web
- **Firebase** – Secure cloud infrastructure
- **Google Gemini AI** – For smart verse recommendations (optional, always user-controlled)
